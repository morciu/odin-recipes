Almond Milk

Description

A recipe for homemade almond milk.

Ingredients

  • 200g raw almonds
  • 2-3 pitted dates
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 liter of water

Steps

  1. Soak almonds in a boul over night. (In the fridge).
  2. Peel each almond individually.
  3. Put all the ingredients in a blender and... BLEND!
  4. In a large bowl or a pot, filter the blended stuff through a cheese cloth.
  5. Pour the milk in a glass bottle and put a cap on it.
