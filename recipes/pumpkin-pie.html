Pumpkin Pie

Description

A recipe for homemade pumpkin pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie pumpkin, halved and seeded
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Lightly oil pumpkin pieces, put them on a baking sheet with insides down.
  3. Bake until soft.
  4. Blend ingredients.
  5. Put the blended filling in a pie crust.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10-15 minutes.
