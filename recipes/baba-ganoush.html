Baba Ganoush

Description

A recipe for homemade baba ganoush.

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/8 teaspoon olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • chili to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven and lightly oil a baking sheet.
  2. Halve eggplant(s), brush with oil and place cut side down on baking sheet.
  3. Roast until soft.
  4. Scoop flesh, drain and place it in a food processor.
  5. Add rest of ingredients and blend everything together.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Place in a fancy bowl and decorate with paprika.
